Beef and Tomato Stew

Description

A hearty beef and tomato soup good for a cold winter night

Ingredients

  • Cubed beef
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Canned beans
  • Vegetable stock
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan with oil
  2. Add the beef and cook until all ready
  3. Add the potatoes, onions and other hard vegetables
  4. Add the tomatoes, beans, stock and other soft vegetables
  5. Add some water depending on how watery you want it
  6. Bring to boil and simmer for 20mins or more
  7. Serve and bonapetit!
