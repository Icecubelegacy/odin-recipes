Minestrone

Description

A hearty tomato based soup good for nourishing the soul

Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Canned beans
  • Vegetable stock
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan with oil
  2. Add the potatoes, onions and other hard vegetables
  3. Once mostly cooked, add the tomatoes, beans, stock and other soft vegetables
  4. Bring to boil and simmer for 20mins or more
  5. Serve and bonapetit!
