Shakshuka

Description

A hearty tomato based soup usually eaten for brunch

Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Canned beans
  • Vegetable stock
  • Parsley
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan with oil
  2. Add the potatoes, onions and other hard vegetables
  3. Once mostly cooked, add the tomatoes, beans, stock and other soft vegetables
  4. Bring to boil and simmer for 20mins or more
  5. Add eggs and pepper
  6. Serve and bonapetit!
